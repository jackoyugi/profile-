Am Jack Oyugi, am an Entreprenuer, Researcher and Junior software developer, my passion for becoming a developer was born out of courosity to solve global challenges though IoT. For this reason I enrolled for a software devepment course at Moringa School. In Moringa School, I got a chance to learn many programming languages in software development from HTML, CSS, JS, JavaScript, Angular, and then majored in Java language. At Moringa School my focus was to graduate as an android developer with interest in frontend and backend android development. The skills and technics I learned at Moringa has equiped me as a full junior android and web developer. Besides being a developer, am also a world class leading innovator. My role here is to help in the teaching of Undergraduate and Postgraduate students majorly in Veterinary Virology Practicals lessons, diagnosis of Veterinary clinical cases brought in the section, and also research in agriculture and veterianry areas.
